TwiTree

TwiTree is an app that allows you to connect with people in very distant areas very quick. TwiTree also allows you to pay a bill while still having your social status going.The service is meant to be able to connect families that otherwise would not be able to communicate at all. Twitree connects the net service Twilio an the paying service of Braintree to bring voice around the world
