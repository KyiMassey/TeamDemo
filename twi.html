TwiTree

Our App is called Twitree. We created it with hopes of connecting family and friends on different sides of the world. The app connects two online programs to bring a much needed service. TwiTree is a combination of the online apps Twilio and Braintree. With this "Dynamic Duo" of a sorts, users are able to ,for a small fee, come in contact with others who they, without Twitree would not be able to come in contact with. We believed this would be successful because we realized communication and staying in touch is key in this changing world.
